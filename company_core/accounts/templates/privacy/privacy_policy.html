We use your information to:

  • Manage your fleet account and service history.

  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., MTO safety records).

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted storage of digital records and secure facilities for physical documents. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.
