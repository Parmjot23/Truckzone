1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how {{ business_name|default:default_business_name }} ("Company", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our website, online parts catalog/store, customer portal, and parts inquiry forms (collectively, the "Platform").

This Privacy Policy is provided by the Company. Any independent software developers, contractors, or technology vendors involved in building or maintaining the Platform do so on behalf of the Company and are not the controller/owner of your personal information for their own independent purposes.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Contact details: Name, email address, phone number, and the details you include in messages.
  • Parts inquiries and quotes: Part numbers, vehicle details (make/model/year, VIN or unit number), photos, and fitment notes you share.
  • Orders and billing: Shipping and billing addresses, tax numbers (if applicable), purchase history, and fleet account details.
  • Customer/portal account details: Account credentials (stored in hashed form), company name (if applicable), and authorized users.
  • Uploads: Photos, documents, and other files you choose to upload (for example, part images or spec sheets).
  • Communications: Messages you send to us by email, forms, portal messaging, or AI chat features (where available).

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device and connection data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers (where available), operating system, and approximate location (derived from IP).
  • Usage data: Pages viewed, actions taken, access times, referring URLs, and diagnostic logs.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: See our Cookies Policy for details.

2.3 Payment information

If you buy parts through our Platform, payment processing may be handled by third-party payment processors. We do not store full payment card numbers on our servers. Payment processors handle your payment information according to their own policies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to parts inquiries, confirm fitment, and provide quotes.
  • Process orders, returns, and warranty requests.
  • Create and manage customer/portal accounts and authenticate users.
  • Prepare invoices, receipts, and account statements where applicable.
  • Communicate with you about orders, availability, pickup or delivery, and support requests.
  • Maintain security, prevent fraud/abuse, and enforce our Terms and policies.
  • Comply with applicable laws, accounting requirements, and regulatory obligations.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With vendors: Partners that help us run the Platform (hosting, email/SMS, analytics, maps, file storage, payments, customer support tools, shipping carriers, and similar providers) under appropriate confidentiality/security obligations.
  • With operational partners: When needed to fulfill your request (for example, parts suppliers or manufacturers).
  • For legal reasons: If required by law, regulation, or legal process; or to protect our rights, safety, and property, and those of our customers.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of all or a portion of the business (subject to standard protections).

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no security measure is perfect, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information as long as necessary to provide the Platform and for legitimate business purposes such as recordkeeping, invoicing/accounting, dispute resolution, and compliance with legal obligations. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of record.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You may have rights and choices regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access and correction: Request access to and correction of certain information we hold about you.
  • Account preferences: Update certain details through your account (if available).
  • Marketing choices: Opt out of marketing communications by using unsubscribe links or contacting us.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and/or certain information, subject to legal and business recordkeeping needs.

8. Children

Our Platform is not intended for children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above will indicate when changes become effective once posted.
