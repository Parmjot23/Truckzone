Work Order Feature

Streamline your jobs, parts, and invoicing with one seamless workflow.

Overview

Our Work Order feature empowers your business to create, assign, and track detailed job tasks while integrating with the inventory system. Employees can log parts usage and progress, and once the task is complete, the system automatically updates inventory and generates invoices.

How It Works

  1. Create a Work Order: Initiate the process by detailing the job requirements and specific instructions.
  2. Assign to an Employee: Allocate the task and enable the employee to access available inventory parts.
  3. Employee Updates: The assigned employee logs parts usage and progress in real time.
  4. Review & Finalize: The business reviews the updated work order, makes any necessary adjustments, and finalizes it.
  5. Automatic Integration: Upon completion, the system automatically updates your inventory and generates the corresponding invoice.

Benefits

  • Efficiency: Eliminate manual entry and reduce operational delays.
  • Accuracy: Automation minimizes errors and ensures data integrity.
  • Transparency: Everyone has access to real-time status updates and inventory levels.
  • Streamlined Workflow: Seamlessly integrates work orders, inventory management, and invoicing.
