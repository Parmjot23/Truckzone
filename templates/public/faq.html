FAQ

Answers to the Most Common Questions

Use these quick answers to plan pickups, confirm availability, and get the right truck and trailer parts fast.

What are your hours of operation?

Hours vary by location. See the location cards on our contact page for daily hours.

Do you carry OEM and aftermarket parts?

Yes. We stock trusted OEM-grade and aftermarket options to meet different fleet budgets and timelines.

Can you help confirm part fitment?

Absolutely. Share your VIN, unit number, or part number and our team will verify the correct match.

Do you offer delivery or pickup?

Yes. We offer same-day pickup and can arrange local delivery based on availability.

How do I set up a fleet account?

Register online or contact the parts desk to set up account pricing and ordering access.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, debit, and offer fleet account billing for qualified businesses with approved credit.

Do you offer quotes for fleet or bulk orders?

Yes. Share your parts list, quantities, and deadlines, and we will provide a fast quote with availability and lead times.

Can you source special-order parts?

We can special-order OEM and aftermarket parts not currently in stock. Lead times vary by brand and supplier.

What types of parts do you carry?

We stock heavy-duty truck and trailer parts including brakes, suspension, lighting, filters, driveline, electrical, and shop supplies.

Do you help with cross-references or part numbers?

Yes. Provide a part number, brand, or spec and we will cross-reference to compatible options.

Can I get updates on order status?

We can provide updates by phone or email. For account customers, invoices and statements are available online.

Do you have warranties on parts?

Warranty coverage depends on the manufacturer. We will explain warranty terms and help with claims when applicable.

What is your return policy?

Returns are accepted on unused items in original packaging. Some special-order or electrical items may be non-returnable.

Do you charge core deposits?

Some components include core charges. Bring the core back for credit based on supplier guidelines.

Can I place orders online?

Yes. Use the storefront to browse, search by brand or category, and submit orders anytime.

Do you offer same-day pickup?

In-stock items are available for same-day pickup. We will confirm timing when your order is placed.

How do invoices and statements work?

Fleet accounts receive invoices per order and statements for monthly balances. Contact the parts desk for access or billing questions.

Still need help?

Our parts desk can confirm fitment, pricing, and availability for your fleet.
