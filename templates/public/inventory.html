Inventory Management

Effortless stock management with real-time updates and smart notifications.

Overview

Our integrated Inventory system lets you create custom product categories, manage suppliers, and record detailed stock transactions. Every time a work order or invoice is generated, the inventory is automatically updated to reflect a stock out, ensuring your stock levels are always current.

Key Features

  • Custom Categories: Organize products into categories that fit your business needs.
  • Supplier Management: Easily track suppliers and link them with your products.
  • Product Creation: Add products with complete details including SKU, description, and cost.
  • Stock Transactions: Record stock ins, stock outs, and adjustments with ease.
  • Automatic Updates: Inventory is updated automatically when products are used in work orders or invoices.
  • Reorder Notifications: Set reorder levels and receive alerts before stock runs low.

How It Works

  1. Create Categories & Suppliers: Set up product categories and record supplier details.
  2. Add Products: Input your products with all relevant details to streamline future transactions.
  3. Manage Stock Movements: Log stock ins for new purchases, stock outs during sales or work orders, and adjustments as needed.
  4. Auto-Sync: Every work order or invoice involving a product triggers an automatic inventory update.
  5. Reorder Alerts: Get notified instantly when stock levels fall below your preset threshold.

Benefits

  • Real-Time Data: Keep your inventory information current at all times.
  • Operational Efficiency: Reduce manual errors and save time with automated updates.
  • Cost-Effective Management: Prevent overstocking or understocking with timely alerts.
  • Seamless Integration: Works perfectly with our Work Order and Invoice features.
